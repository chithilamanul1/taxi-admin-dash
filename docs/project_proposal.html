Overhaul Proposal

Prepared for Airport Taxi Tours

1. Executive Summary

This proposal outlines a comprehensive digital transformation for the Airport Taxi Tours platform. Our goal is to evolve the current system into a world-class travel and transport management solution that offers unmatched user experience, precise pricing logic, and a premium aesthetic that builds trust and authority.

By integrating multi-currency support, advanced tour categorization, and intelligent waiting charge calculations, the platform will cater to both local and international travelers with seamless efficiency.

2. Project Objectives

Global Reach

Support major international currencies (USD, EUR, INR) alongside LKR to facilitate transparent pricing for global customers.

Operational Excellence

Automate complex calculations like multi-stop routes, waiting charges, and dynamic vehicle rates.

Unique Branding

Implement a "wow-factor" UI that distinguishes Airport Taxi Tours from generic competitors.

4. Technical Architecture

The platform will be built using a modern, high-performance stack:

  • Frontend: Next.js 15 (App Router) for SEO and speed.
  • Styling: Tailwind CSS with custom premium tokens.
  • Backend: Node.js with Mongoose (MongoDB) for robust data handling.
  • Authentication: Next-Auth + Firebase for secure user management.
  • Maps: OSRM Engine & Leaflet/Mapbox for route visualization.

5. Unique UI/UX Concept

We are moving away from standard templates. The new UI will feature:

Glassmorphism UI

Translucent cards and frosted-glass effects for a modern, futuristic feel.

Dynamic Micro-Animations

Smooth transitions between booking steps and hover effects that make the site feel "alive".

Dark Mode First

A premium "Night" theme option (Enterprise Dark) for a high-end visual experience.

6. Advanced Booking Logic

The new logic will handle:

- Complex Distances: Automated KM calculation via OSRM.
- Waiting Charges: Special rates for Colombo, Kalutara, and Seeduwa zones ($100-$200/hr).
- Quotations: Real-time PDF/HTML generation of trip estimates.

7. Tours & Safaris

Tours are no longer just "rentals". They are curated experiences.

Day Tours

Quick escapes to Galle, Kandy, or Sigiriya.

Safari

Integrated bookings for Yala, Udawalawe, and Minneriya.

8. Third-Party Integrations
