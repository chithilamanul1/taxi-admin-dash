# Sampath Bank IPG - Credential Retrieval Guide

You already have an approved Sampath Bank IPG account. Now you need to locate your credentials to enable online payments on your website.

You need to provide your web developer with these **3 items**:

| Credential | What It Looks Like |
|------------|-------------------|
| **Merchant ID** | Usually a numeric code (e.g., `123456789`) |
| **Terminal ID** | Usually a short alphanumeric code (e.g., `TERM01`) |
| **Secret Key** | A long string of letters and numbers used for security |

---

## üìç Where to Find Your Credentials

### Option 1: Check Your Welcome Email/Letter

When your IPG account was approved, Sampath Bank should have sent you a **welcome letter** or **email** containing your credentials. Look for:

- Subject line mentioning "IPG" or "Payment Gateway"
- A document titled "Merchant Integration Details"

### Option 2: Log into the Sampath IPG Merchant Portal

1. Go to the **Sampath Bank Merchant Portal** (the URL was provided in your welcome email).
2. Log in with your credentials.
3. Navigate to **Settings** or **Integration** or **API Keys** section.
4. Your Merchant ID, Terminal ID, and Secret Key should be displayed or downloadable.

### Option 3: Contact Sampath Bank Directly

If you cannot locate your credentials:

1. Call the **Sampath Bank Merchant Services Hotline**.
2. Provide your business name and NIC for verification.
3. Request your **Merchant ID**, **Terminal ID**, and **Secret Key** to be resent.
